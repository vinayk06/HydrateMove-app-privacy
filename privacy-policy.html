If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to restriction: you can ask us to limit how we process your data.
  • Right to data portability: you can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Our legal basis for processing your data is typically your consent or our legitimate interest in operating and improving the App. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address listed below. We will respond within 30 days.
