📱 About the App

Hydrate & Move helps you build two of the most important daily habits — drinking enough water and staying active throughout the day. Set your goals, get timely reminders, and track your progress all in one place. The app is completely free.

✨ Features

💧 Water Intake Tracking

Log each time you drink water and monitor your daily hydration progress toward your personal goal.

👟 Step Tracking

Track your daily steps and stay on top of your movement goals throughout the day.

⏰ Active Hours

Set your active hours in Settings so reminders only appear when you're awake and on the go — not during sleep or off hours.

🔔 Water Reminders

Choose your preferred reminder interval (e.g., every 30 min, 1 hour, 2 hours) so you never forget to hydrate.

🏃 Move Reminders

Get nudged to get up and move at your chosen interval throughout the day to reduce long periods of inactivity.

⚙️ How to Use Settings

Open the Settings tab in the app to customize your experience:

Setting Active Hours

Tap Active Hours and set your start and end time for the day. Reminders will only fire within this window.

Water Reminder Interval

Under Water Reminders, select how often you'd like to be reminded to drink water. Options range from every 30 minutes to every few hours.

Move Reminder Interval

Under Move Reminders, select how often you'd like a nudge to get up and move. You can set a different interval than your water reminders.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I not receiving reminders?

Make sure notifications are enabled for Hydrate & Move in your iPhone's Settings → Notifications. Also check that your active hours are set correctly in the app.

How do I reset my daily progress?

Your water and step counts reset automatically at midnight each day.

Can I change my reminder intervals anytime?

Yes — go to Settings in the app and update your water or move reminder intervals whenever you like. Changes take effect immediately.

Does the app work in the background?

Reminders are delivered as notifications, so they work even when the app is closed, as long as notifications are enabled.

Is the app free?

Yes, Hydrate & Move is completely free to download and use.
